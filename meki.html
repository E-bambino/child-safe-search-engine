Online Addiction!

Here are some tips

Well, imagine that you really like drinking apple juice. You love it so much that you start drinking it all the time, even when you're not thirsty. You start to feel like you can't go without it and you want to drink it all the time. That's how some people feel when they use the internet a lot. They might start using it all the time, even when they don't really need to. They might feel like they can't go without it and want to use it all the time. That's called online addiction, and it's important to remember that just like drinking too much apple juice isn't good for you, using the internet too much can also be harmful. It's important to take breaks and do other things that you enjoy too.

Here are some of online abuse symptoms

Staying longer in the internet than originally intended by the parent.

Sneaking online when they think an adult or the parent is not watching.

Telling lies about the amount of time you spent online.

The child disobeys set internet rules set by the parent.

Staying up all night just for the sake of being online.

When you disobey set of internet rules set by the parent.

Here are some ways to stop online addiction !

Do not turn the computer without a specific purpose.

Set internet access time through a family discussion and the parent to also do a follow up to ensure the child stick to the rules.

Parents to make use of time management software to ensure that children are not staying on the internet for long hours.

Parents to spend more time with the children on outdoors activities apart from using the internet.
